Privacy Policy

GuardIan – Parental Controls and GuardIan Child
Developed by Corsair Labs
Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Corsair Labs collects, uses, and protects information when you use the GuardIan apps.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you sign in.
  • Child device location: Real-time GPS location collected on the child device and shared exclusively with the linked parent account.
  • Device information: Device name, battery level, and online/offline status.
  • App usage data: Information about apps on the child device for time-limit and monitoring features.
  • Push notification tokens: Used only to deliver alerts between parent and child accounts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display the child's real-time location to the linked parent.
  • Enforce app time limits configured by the parent.
  • Deliver SOS alerts and time-extension requests between parent and child.
  • Authenticate users securely via Firebase Authentication.

3. Background Location

The GuardIan Child app uses background location to keep the parent informed of the child's whereabouts. This data is transmitted only to the linked parent account and is not used for advertising or shared with any third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Data is shared only between a linked parent and child account. We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Messaging) and Google Maps. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Location data is overwritten in real time and is not stored historically. Account data is retained until you request deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

The GuardIan Child app is installed by a parent or guardian. All data collection on the child device is visible to and controlled by the parent account.

7. Security

Data is stored in Google Firebase, protected by Firebase Security Rules that restrict access to the linked parent and child accounts only.
